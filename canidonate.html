Am I eligible to donate blood ?

Eligibility for donating blood

  1. You must be 18 -60 years old and have a minimum weight of 50Kg can donate blood.
  2. You should be healthy, fit and not be suffering from any transmittable diseases can donate blood.
  3. Have not donated blood in the last 56 days.
  4. One should have a normal body temperature at the time of donation.
